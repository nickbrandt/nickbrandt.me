Skillset

Design has been a focus of mine since I can remember, and along with that web development also became of quick interest when it started taking off in the 90's. Although my schooling revolved mainly around the design aspect, I found myself keeping up with front-end development as well. This not only allows me the capabilities to build out the designs I produce, but it's also extremely helpful when planning out the layout and structure of my work. Understanding how products are built provides me with that added perspective and assists in creating a smooth transition from concept to implementation.

The Process

Strategy

Behind every great website lies a great stategy. Through UX best practices, wireframing and team collaboration I work to construct sites that address each clients specific needs and goals.

Design

Design can play a large part in how a brand is perceived by others. I work to craft products that appear as natural extensions of brands, while pushing to keep them as fresh and leading edge as possible.

Development

Development can make or break a site. I understand the importance of efficient, clean code and strive to build experiences that are both fast and fluid from one device to the next.
