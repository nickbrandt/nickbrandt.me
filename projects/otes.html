Project Overview

Orange Tree is a market leader and premeir provider of employment screening solutions to corporations across America. Their website was outdated and in need of help, and since they had recently updated their branding it was the perfect opportunity to start fresh. They needed a site that would be easy to manage and distribute resources, while updating the look to be brighter and more engaging.

I put together a bold design for the home page, pulling some vibrant colors from their palette that help to bring energy to the page. The interior pages of the site are more subtle and clean, with pops of color accenting the content. We utilized Drupal to manage the content, making it simple for the client to update and distribute resources. One of the custom pieces we put together for the site was a document repository in which the administrator could distribute files based on whether or not the user had an account with the site or not. This also contained a backend area that showed data related to who downloaded what files. The project went smoothly from start to finish, and the client loved the final result.
