Project Overview

Westwood Professional Services ia a multi-disciplined land surveying and engineering firm with it's headquarters based out of Minneapolis. They were freshening their look with a brand agency in the area (Level) and came to August Ash for a new site to go along with the rebrand. They wanted to keep the design portion with Level, since they had been working on the rest of the collateral, but looked to us for the strategy and to build the finished product.

I worked with the client to develop a strategy for their site that aligned with their new vision, and created wire frames to provide to Level in which to base the designs. Throughout the planning process I was also able to direct the client to different design decisions, such as color coding the different pieces of the site to visually distinguish which market they were regarding.

The designs Level put together were fantastic, and I worked with them in fine tuning them for web use. During development I also had the task of transitioning the desktop designs we were provided for a responsive framework. The final product turned out great, and not only did the site look amazing, but it flowed from one device to the next seamlessly.
