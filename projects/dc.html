Project Overview

I had the great opportunity of working on a web application for Deluxe Corporation that would be utilized in their annual Deluxe Exchange conference. What really made this project exciting was it's free range - they were looking for something to generate ideas for future paths of their business, and they wanted it new and exciting. Other than that, they were open to ideas.

A fellow developer and I put together a concept based around the idea of an interactive mind map. We began with a list of categories they branch the ideas off of, which we categorized by color and icon. Through the use of size relationships we were able to create a heiarcy throughout the application. Users had the ability to add ideas, sub-thoughts that can branch off those ideas, as well as add comments or "likes" to each idea. Using the application, users were able to quickly visualize different aspects of the data. Ideas that gathered more attention (more likes) visually grew in size, helping to show some of the live statistics throughout the event.

Another touch that was added to the application to give it that fun and exciting feel was the subtle animations. The ideas very slowly drift along the screen as you are using it. The user also has the ability to touch and drag the different ideas and categories across the screen, arranging them however they want. The toolbar remains constant on the left, and transitions seemlessly when moving throughout the application to display additional content.
