Project Overview

Kathleen rule is an up-and-coming pen and ink artist working out of Minneapolis - she also happens to be my fiance :). So when the need came for her to have a website, it just made sense that I be the one to do it.

Since I had never used it at the time, I decided to take the opportunity to learn a new CMS - Squarespace. Using their developer platform I was able to customize a site for Katie that gave it her own look and feel, while taking advantage of the many backend capabilities of the platform.

I kept the design of the site neutral and clean, keeping the focus on the artwork and offering a gallery-like feel. Sites like these are great to work on simply due to the freedom I get in trying out new techniques and practices.
