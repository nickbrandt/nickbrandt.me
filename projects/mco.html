Project Overview

North Mallow & Co, previously Campfire Desserts, is a startup gourmet marshmallow company based out of Minneapolis. They had been focusing on s'mores catering for the beginning of the business and were looking to expand to retail. Although they were known for their marshmallows and s'mores, they eventually wanted to expand to other areas within the market as well. What they wanted was a rebrand - a new name and a fresh, modern look to go along with it.

We put our heads together, and a few months later North Mallow & Co was born. I kept the design of the logo simple and playful, keeping the focus on "mallow" while giving it a modern feel. I created two versions - a stacked and horizontal for different use cases.

Since we were developing the site during the busy season for the company, I also designed and developed a splash page that they could direct customers to until the site was complete. For the design of the splash and main site I worked to keep things clean and fun, while pushing the gourmet feeling with the use of large photography and fine details. I focused the color scheme around earth-tones and utilized textures you would relate to the outoors and camping throughout the design.

This project is currently in development and there will be more to come soon!
